Our Legacy

Welcome to the official repository website of Amrita University's Engineering College. Founded in 2003, our institution is a proud member of the Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham educational network. Under the compassionate guidance of our Chancellor, Sri Mata Amritanandamayi Devi (Amma), we have earned a global reputation for excellence in engineering and technology education.

Academic Excellence

Amrita University's is committed to providing students with a world-class education. We offer rigorous academic programs that build a strong foundation in engineering principles and prepare students to excel in the fast-paced world of technology. Our dedicated faculty, cutting-edge facilities, and a focus on research and innovation have consistently placed us among the top-ranked engineering colleges globally.
